Once you have two (or more) models, you should choose at least four pieces of text -- texts not used in your models' creation -- that you would be interested in classifying according to your models.

For example, you could see if

your Writ1 paper (or thesis) is more like W.S. or J.K.R.
the Chicago Tribune is more like the NYT or the WSJ
Bart Simpson is more like Michael Bluth or Raj Koothrappali
or any other comparison you would like to brainstorm.
